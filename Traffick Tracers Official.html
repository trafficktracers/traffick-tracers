Trafficking Awareness & Indicators

Physical Indicators
  • Unexplained injuries or signs of physical abuse
  • Malnourished appearance or poor physical condition
  • Inappropriate clothing for weather or setting
  • Evidence of branding or tattooing
  • Signs of medical neglect
Behavioral Indicators
  • Fearful, anxious, or paranoid behavior
  • Submissive or coached responses
  • Avoids eye contact or social interaction
  • Shows signs of trauma or PTSD
  • Demonstrates unusually fearful reactions
Control Indicators
  • No access to personal documents
  • Restricted movement or communication
  • Monitored constantly by others
  • Limited knowledge of whereabouts
  • No control over money or identification
Living Conditions
  • Lives in overcrowded or unsuitable conditions
  • Multiple people in cramped spaces
  • Restricted access to food or basic necessities
  • Windows covered or secured from inside
  • Evidence of confinement or security measures
